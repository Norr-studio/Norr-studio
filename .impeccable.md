## Design Context

### Users
Startup founders and SMEs in Finland and the Nordics. They're evaluating whether to hire Norr Studio vs. a generic agency or freelancer. They want fast delivery, high craft, and a partner who understands modern product design — not just "web design." They browse on desktop, may check on mobile. They're design-literate enough to notice quality.

### Brand Personality
**Deliberate. Beautiful. Forward.**
Not warm, not friendly, not approachable in a generic agency way. Precise and confident — the feeling of a Scandinavian studio that also ships at Vercel/Framer velocity. Understated rather than loud.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark editorial, software-quality craft applied to agency output. Heavy influence from Framer and Vercel's own sites: Geist typography, black backgrounds, rich imagery showcasing actual work, minimal UI chrome.

The single accent color `#00E5A0` (teal-mint) appears only on black or near-black backgrounds — never on light surfaces. It is used sparingly: primary CTAs, key stat numbers, active states. It should feel like a signal, not wallpaper.

**Anti-references:** Generic colorful agency sites, freelancer portfolios, template-looking layouts, anything that feels like it was built with a website builder.

### Typography
- **Primary font**: Geist (weights 300–800) — used for both display and body
- Headings: bold (700–800), tight tracking, large fluid sizes via `clamp()`
- Body: regular (400) or light (300), generous line-height (1.65–1.75)
- No italic headings — weight and size carry hierarchy, not slant

### Colors
- Background: `#0a0a0a` (near-black, very slightly warm)
- Accent / brand: `#00E5A0` — only on dark backgrounds
- Text primary: `#ffffff`
- Text secondary: `rgba(255,255,255,0.5–0.6)`
- Text tertiary / labels: `rgba(255,255,255,0.25–0.35)`
- Borders / dividers: `rgba(255,255,255,0.05–0.08)`

### Design Principles
1. **Product over portfolio** — looks like a product company's site, not a typical agency
2. **Restraint with the accent** — `#00E5A0` earns attention by being rare; never use it decoratively
3. **Typography does the work** — large Geist headings carry hierarchy; decoration is minimal
4. **Nordic deliberateness** — no filler sections; every element justifies its existence
5. **Dark always** — the brand color only ever appears against black or near-black backgrounds
